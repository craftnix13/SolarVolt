Frequently Asked

Everything you need to know before going solar.

Most homeowners save 70–90% on their electricity bills. A typical 5kW system generates 20–22 units per day, which covers an average family's consumption. With net metering, excess units are sent to the grid and you earn credits from MSEDCL.

Under PM Surya Ghar Muft Bijli Yojana, you get ₹30,000–₹78,000 subsidy for residential systems up to 3kW. Commercial systems also get accelerated depreciation benefit of 40%. SolarVolt handles the entire subsidy application for you at no extra charge.

Residential installations are completed within 2–3 days. Commercial projects take 5–15 days depending on system size. The entire process from survey to commissioning takes 2–4 weeks including net meter application and grid connection.

Solar panels still generate electricity on cloudy days, typically at 20–40% capacity. With net metering, units exported during sunny days are credited against your night and monsoon consumption. Battery backup options ensure 24/7 power supply.

Premium solar panels last 30+ years. We provide a 25-year linear performance warranty — panels will produce at minimum 80% of rated power after 25 years. Inverters have a 10-year warranty with free replacement included in our premium packages.

Yes! We offer 0% interest EMI for 12–36 months through our banking partners including HDFC, SBI, and Bajaj Finserv. Many customers find that their EMI amount is less than their current electricity bill — making it cash-flow positive from day one.
